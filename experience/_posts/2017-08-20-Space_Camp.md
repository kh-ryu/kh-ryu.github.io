I worked as the principal director and organizer for the 2017 Space Challenger camp, hosted by SNU rocket team HANARO and supported by the Korea Foundation for the Advancement of Science & Creativity (KFASC). As the principal director, I designed the main parts of the camp and managed a proposal for a rocket education camp to receive funding from KFASC. I also participated in camp as head mentor, providing rocket education and leading students.

![Camp](/assets/images/camp1.jpg){: width="48%" height="48%"}![Camp](/assets/images/camp2.jpg){: width="48%" height="48%"}

The camp was organized with primary rocketry education, SolidWorks tutorial, model rockets, and launch experiments. 

![Camp](/assets/images/camp3.jpg){: width="48%" height="48%"}

It was fascinating to organize such an extensive program. Moreover, helping students with my experience and knowledge was an invaluable experience.
