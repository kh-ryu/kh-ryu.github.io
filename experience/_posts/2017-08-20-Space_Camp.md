I worked as a main director and organizer for 2017 Space Challenger camp, hosted by SNU rocket team HANARO and supported by Korea Foundation for the Advancement of Science & Creativity (KFASC). As a main director, I designed main parts of the camp and managed proposal for rocket education camp to receive funding from KFASC. I also participated camp as head mentor, providing rocket education and leading students.

![Camp](/assets/images/camp1.jpg){: width="48%" height="48%"}![Camp](/assets/images/camp2.jpg){: width="48%" height="48%"}

The camp was organized with basic rocketry education, SolidWorks tutorial, making model rockets, and launch experiments. 

![Camp](/assets/images/camp3.jpg){: width="48%" height="48%"}

It was very exciting to organize such a big program. Moreover, helping students with my expirience and knowledge was invaluable experience.
