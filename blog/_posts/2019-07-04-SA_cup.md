Spaceport America cup is the world's largest intercollegiate rocket engineering conference and competition   
Students and faculties gathers in Spaceport America (where virgin galactic launches their flights) and fly their rockets.   
I attended 2019 SA Cup as a member of HANARO, Seoul National University student rocket club.   

![Rockets](/assets/images/rockets.jpg)

Personally, it was great experience and gave me invaluable memory. Not everyone is able to travel to another country and have a project experience with friends who share passion about rockets and space. Even though we had to work overnight in Airbnb and its garage, the time I spent with my teammates was one of the happiest days of my undergraduate life.
