There are numerous online resources related to Robotics, Reinforcement Learning (RL), and Control research. Below is a list of research websites, blogs, and other valuable resources for studying these topics.

* [Lil'Log](https://lilianweng.github.io)
Lilian Weng is an AI researcher focused on AI safety and alignment at OpenAI. Her blog covers a variety of topics including Reinforcement Learning, Transformers, Diffusion Models, and LLM/VLM.

* [Stanford AI Lab Blog](https://ai.stanford.edu/blog/about/)
The Stanford AI Lab (SAIL) Blog features recent work by SAIL students and faculty. Their research spans computer vision, robotics, machine learning, and reinforcement learning.

* [BAIR Blog](https://bair.berkeley.edu/blog/about/)
Similar to the SAIL Blog, the BAIR Blog showcases research from the Berkeley AI Research (BAIR) lab, one of the leading AI research institutions known for its groundbreaking work. Go Bears!
