As an Undergraduate Research Program (URP). We developed a Laser Ignition Sounding Rocket Launch System. Our contribution is
* Developed a laser system for igniting solid rocket propellant (KNSB)
* Developed a sounding rocket lauch pad with controllable launch angle
* Conduct a structure analysis of the launch pad
* Integrated above ignition process and launch pad to a launch system

Following is abstract of our project paper.   

Abstract: This paper describes the processes of establishing and standardizing launching system for safe and stable launch of sounding rocket. The launching system is as important as the scientific rocket itself in performing its mission. However, at present, most unlicensed launch systems are used by the undergraduates. This problem may adversely affect the performance of the rocket, and also can lead to safety accidents. In this research, the laser igniter was applied to the launch system to enable easy and stable ignition. In addition, guidelines are suggested for the development by establishing and standardizing the scientific rocket launch system.   

Laser system consists of 5W laser with wavelength 450nm. Plano convex lens and plano concave lens are used for focal length control.
