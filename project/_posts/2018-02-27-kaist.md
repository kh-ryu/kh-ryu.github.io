**Abstract**: I participated SNU/KAIST Sounding Rocket Development Training Program in Feb, 2018. I received two weeks of intesified education on rocket launch vehicle and space systems design. In the program, I learned to calculate rocket trajectory based on a in-house 6DOF MATLAB software (LAFLAS). With this software, I designed launch vehicle inserting 1U Cubesat to LEO.

-----------------

I attended Sounding Rocket Development Training Program from Rocket Lab, KAIST. The program consists of sounding rocket design, hybrid propellant processing, and rocket industry tours.

![Geese_crossing](/assets/images/geese_crossing.jpg){: width="40%" height="40%"}
![SNUKA2](/assets/images/SNUKA.jpg){: width="40%" height="40%"}

Launch Flight Analysis Software ([LAFLAS](http://sai.kaist.ac.kr/saihomepage/?page_id=271)) is rocket flight simulation developed in Strategic Aerospace Initiative, KAIST. In the program, we learned how to use this software for simulating launch vehicles for LEO.

![Simulation](/assets/images/rocket_simulation.jpg)

For the final project, I designed launch vehicle capable of inserting 1U cubesat to LEO. I verified my design with the LAFLAS simulation.
